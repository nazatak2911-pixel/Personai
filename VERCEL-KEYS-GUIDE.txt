PERSONNA AI - HOW TO ENABLE THE CHATBOT ON YOUR LIVE WEBSITE (VERCEL)
================================================================

Currently, your chatbot works on your LOCAL computer because of the .env file.
But for the LIVE website (the GitHub link) to work, you must tell Vercel your keys.

Follow these 3 simple steps:

1. GO TO VERCEL DASHBOARD:
   - Go to https://vercel.com/dashboard
   - Click on your "PersonnaAI" project.

2. OPEN SETTINGS:
   - Click on the "Settings" tab at the top.
   - Click on "Environment Variables" in the left sidebar.

3. ADD YOUR KEYS:
   - Add the first one:
     - Key: VITE_OPENROUTER_API_KEY
     - Value: (Paste your sk-or-v1-... key here)
     - Click "Add".
   - Add the second one:
     - Key: VITE_GEMINI_API_KEY
     - Value: (Paste your AIzaSyB... key here)
     - Click "Add".

4. REDEPLOY:
   - Go to the "Deployments" tab at the top.
   - Click the three dots (...) next to your latest deployment.
   - Click "Redeploy".

THAT'S IT! Your live website will now have a working AI bot! 🚀📱✨
